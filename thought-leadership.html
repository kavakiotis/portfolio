TedX Talk: Buildings - The Friends We Never Knew We Had

In 2021, I delivered a TedX talk exploring the relationship between humans and the buildings we inhabit. The talk examines how smart building technology can transform our built environment from passive shelter into active partners in our wellbeing and productivity.

🎥

TedX Talk Video

"Buildings: The Friends We Never Knew We Had"

View on LinkedIn

Key themes explored:

  • How buildings shape human behavior and wellbeing in ways we rarely notice
  • The potential of IoT and AI to make buildings responsive to human needs
  • The environmental imperative: buildings account for 40% of global energy consumption
  • Moving from "smart buildings" (technology-centric) to "wise buildings" (human-centric)
