Building $24M Partnership Pipeline

Head of Enlighted Europe | Siemens | 2022-2023

Challenge: Enlighted (Siemens' IoT lighting and building intelligence platform) needed to scale European operations beyond direct sales capacity. The European market had complex regional dynamics and required local partner expertise to reach enterprise customers effectively.

Approach: Developed a comprehensive Partner Playbook based on Miller Heiman strategic selling methodology. The playbook covered four critical areas: partner recruitment and qualification, enablement and certification programs, deal registration and pipeline management, and co-marketing frameworks.

Results:

  • Generated $24M partner-sourced pipeline (800% growth from baseline)
  • Secured $2.5M largest global SaaS deal in Siemens Enlighted history
  • 91% success rate on global partnership strategy implementation
  • Established first European Support Service contract (recurring revenue model)
  • Werner von Siemens Global Award nominee for University of Birmingham implementation

Record-Breaking Sales Performance

Head of Enlighted Northern Europe | Siemens | 2020-2022

Challenge: Northern Europe was an underperforming territory for Enlighted's building IoT solutions. The region had strong competition from established local players and limited market awareness of Siemens' smart building capabilities.

Approach: Implemented a three-part strategy: (1) Territory segmentation based on vertical potential and competitive positioning, (2) Partner network development across Nordic markets, (3) Introduction of Digital Services to create recurring revenue streams alongside project-based sales.

Results:

  • FY20: +12.5% above target (despite pandemic disruption)
  • FY21: +300% above target (highest in division history)
  • FY22: +20% above target (sustained performance)
  • Introduced first Digital Services offerings for Enlighted in Northern Europe
  • Expanded partner network across Nordic markets driving revenue growth

AI-Powered Sales Transformation

Global Head of Customer Success & Sales Engineering | Siemens | 2024-2025

Challenge: As Global Head, I was tasked with transforming customer success operations to drive 20% YoY ARR growth from a $6.5M base. Traditional approaches weren't scaling effectively across global operations.

Approach: Pioneered an AI-powered transformation program that integrated multiple tools: ZoomInfo and Bombora for intent data, Outreach for engagement automation, and AI assistants (OpenAI, Perplexity) for research synthesis and content generation. Built frameworks for AI-assisted proposal development and customer communications.

Results:

  • 40% reduction in sales cycle through AI-assisted qualification and proposal generation
  • 35% increase in conversion rates from AI-improved targeting and personalization
  • Designed global CSM Transformation program targeting 20% YoY ARR growth
  • Recognized internally as digital transformation benchmark
  • Framework adopted by other Siemens divisions as best practice

Customer Success Excellence

Head of Building Performance & Sustainability | Siemens | 2017-2020

Challenge: Establish and lead the first Energy Services Company (ESCO) at Siemens UK. Energy services require long-term customer relationships and guaranteed performance outcomes, making customer success critical to business viability.

Approach: Built customer success frameworks focused on proactive value delivery rather than reactive support. Implemented quarterly business reviews, energy performance tracking dashboards, and customer health scoring. Established cross-functional collaboration between delivery, engineering, and customer teams.

Results:

  • 100% customer retention across entire portfolio
  • 80% NPS (highest in division)
  • 100% profitable project delivery
  • ESCO achieved break-even in Year 3
  • Won Gold and Silver awards for Customer First and Innovation
  • Sold and implemented first carbon neutrality project at Siemens UK
